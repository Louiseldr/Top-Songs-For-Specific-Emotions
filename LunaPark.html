Luna Park

Luna Park also known by locals as "The Clown Face" is famous for it's large Clown like appearance that almost "eats you" as you walk in. With fun amusement rides, carnival games, and food to eat, Luna Park can leave you and your family with happy memories for years to come. There are two Luna Parks, One locted in Sydney, Australia, and one located in Melbourne, Australia.
