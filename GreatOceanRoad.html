Great Ocean Road

Great Ocean Road, is a road in Australia most famous for it's ability to see the amazing Twelve apostles. This road offers beautiful views along with the spectacular ocean. The twelve apostles are rocks that were formed by erosion that seem to stand alone in the ocean. There are 12 across Great Ocean Road. Take a long drive and have fun!
