    Bondi Beach

    Bondi Beach located in New South Wales, Sydney, is home to surfers, tanners, families, and many more throughout the year. Although most popular during the summer months, Bondi Beach is open all year. You can surf the beautiful waves, sunbathe, build sand castles, and much more. Bondi Beach is one of the most popular beaches visited throughout the year and is known by all the Australian locals.
